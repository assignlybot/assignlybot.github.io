Teachers Commands

/teachers /assign /announcement /list /mplay

/teachers

This slash command initiates the verification process for teachers. It is a mandatory first step to confirm their identity. Once verified, teachers gain access to all features and resources available.

/assign

This command allows teachers to allocate tasks or assignments to students. Upon activation, the bot will notify students of their pending assignments, ensuring they are reminded of their responsibilities. It streamlines the process of task distribution and helps maintain organized communication between teachers and students.

/announcement

This command allows teachers to broadcast announcements to students. “Study Pal!” will notify students of the upcoming announcement without specifying a time duration. It’s a convenient way to ensure all students are aware of important information.

/list

This command allows teachers to quickly view a list of students who have joined the Discord server. It generates an Excel file that categorizes students based on their sections, making it easy to identify who is present and who is missing from the server. This feature is particularly useful for managing class participation and ensuring all students are connected.

/mplay

Use this command to play your favorite music in a shared session. Simply enter the name of the track, and the bot will stream it for you and other students to enjoy together. Join in and listen to the tunes as a group!
