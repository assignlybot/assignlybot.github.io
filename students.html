Student Commands

/students /to-do /graphicorganizer /mplay /pal

/students

This slash command initiates the verification process for students. It is a mandatory first step to confirm their identity. Once verified, students gain access to all features and resources available. It acts as a gatekeeper, maintaining the integrity and exclusivity of the botâ€™s functionalities.

/to-do

Use this command to view your current and overdue tasks. It provides a quick overview of all the assignments you need to focus on, ensuring you stay on top of your workload and deadlines. Perfect for managing your study schedule efficiently!

/graphicorganizer

This optional command allows you to create a graphic organizer by simply inputting a value. The bot will generate the selected type of graphic organizer based on your input, streamlining the process of organizing information visually. Ideal for students and educators looking to enhance learning and note-taking.

/mplay

Use this command to play your favorite music in a shared session. Simply enter the name of the track, and the bot will stream it for you and other students to enjoy together. Join in and listen to the tunes as a group!

/pal

Use this command to inquire about any topic, and receive an informative response along with a source link for further exploration. Perfect for quick access to knowledge on-the-go!
